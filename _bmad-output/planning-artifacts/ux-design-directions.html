✅ How to Evaluate Design Directions

Layout Intuitiveness
Which information hierarchy feels most natural? How easily do you scan the list?
Interaction Style
Does the interaction feel responsive and satisfying? Can you imagine marking tasks done?
Visual Weight
Does the visual density match your brand? Spacious or compact?
Navigation Clarity
Can you understand where tasks go? Is the visual hierarchy clear?
Emotional Response
Which direction makes you feel confident, accomplished, calm? Which feels modern?
Implementation Fit
Which direction would be easiest to build with Tailwind? Most maintainable?
